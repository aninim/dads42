# Dads'42

## Product

**Vision:** Dark, editorial, competence-focused fatherhood community. Counter to the "gentle parenting" aesthetic. The name comes from Douglas Adams's "42" — the answer to life, the universe, and everything. Nobody knew the question. Fatherhood is exactly like that.

**Platform:** dads42.com (Squarespace) + Skool (skool.com/dads42-3659)

**Key competitor:** Tom Piccirilli (@the.dad.vibes) — owns the gentle parenting / emotional regulation lane. Dads'42 owns the opposite lane.

**Intellectual pillars:**
1. **Science** — Anna Machin, *The Life of Dad* (brain rewiring, evolutionary fatherhood)
2. **Philosophy** — Marcus Aurelius, *Meditations* (accountability, showing up, the standard)
3. **Psychology** — Alfred Adler (masculine protest, cooperation over domination)
4. **Daily Practice** — Ryan Holiday, *The Daily Dad*
5. **Origin Story** — Douglas Adams, *Hitchhiker's Guide* (the name, the in-group)

### Brand Voice (5 Pillars)

1. **Direct** — Say the thing. No softening, no five disclaimers.
2. **Grounded** — Every claim has roots (science, philosophy, lived experience).
3. **Honest** — Don't perform. Say hard things with respect, not judgment.
4. **Earned Warmth** — Warmth from being real, not from being gentle.
5. **Philosophical Weight** — Draw from Aurelius, Machin, history. Occasionally elevate, never lecture.

**Voice in one line:** "A philosopher and a father walk into a locker room. One of them is Marcus Aurelius."

**Copy bank — USE:** self-guided, grounded, built for this, real conversation, tribe/room, figured it out, the weight, blueprint/manual, intentional  
**Copy bank — AVOID:** lost/confused, balanced/centered, you can do it!, safe space, community/group, healed/processed, journey, mindful, you're doing amazing

### Content Strategy

**Funnel:** Social (IG/TikTok/FB/X) → Email list → Skool community → Paid products

**6 content angles:**
- 🔥 Identity / Ego — makes dads feel seen
- 😂 Dark Humor — relatable chaos, gets shared at 11pm
- ⚡ Hot Take — challenges mainstream gentle parenting advice
- 💔 Raw / Confessional — vulnerability that builds trust
- 🛠️ Tactical Win — one thing a dad can do TODAY
- 🐺 Us vs. Them — in-group energy that makes Skool feel like a tribe

**Weekly cadence (Mon–Fri, Sat optional, Sun off):**
- Mon: Identity/Ego reel or written
- Tue: Dark humor reel or carousel
- Wed: Tactical win carousel
- Thu: Confessional talking head or written
- Fri: Hot take or Us vs. Them
- Sat: Skool discussion prompt

**Content production: 2 sessions/week (~2 hrs total):**
- Session 1 (Tue/Wed ~75 min): Brain dump → record raw source → extract assets
- Session 2 (Thu ~60 min): Format + polish → schedule → feed pain bank
