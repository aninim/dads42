**Capturing Magic: How to Create Moments You'll Cherish Forever ✨**
What's a "magic moment" to you? For me, it's that thing your kids do that moves your emotional needle, that makes your subconscious grab your phone to capture a picture or video.
These moments are fleeting, never quite the same as you witnessed them.
But what if we could *create* those moments and then capture them to relive and share? 🤔
Let me tell you about one of my "made" magic moments 🖼️👇🏽
It was just another Saturday afternoon, with nothing special planned. Maybe that's why I felt the urge to take the boys for a walk.
We headed to a patch of fields near our home to wander and watch the sun setting.
I don't often get uninterrupted quality time with my kids. That's why I like taking them to nature, for some positive "distractions" like pecan trees and each other.
I try to soak up as much of it as I can, especially that feeling of connection.
After picking every leaf and examining the insects, we sat down on the earth, each with his collection of sticks (and a shared lollipop!), quietly contemplating our findings under the soft setting sun🌅.
Yes, it was somewhat orchestrated. I wanted these hours to be enjoyed forever whenever I look at that picture again.
I'm looking at it now, and it brings back those feelings. Both grandmothers printed and framed that picture!
It's very powerful!
