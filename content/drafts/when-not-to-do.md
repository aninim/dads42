# When not to do
🟥Ever feel lost when your kid throws a tantrum or just being "impossible"? When you don't know what to do next, and your inner voice goes silent?
🟦You're not alone!
🟨It's tempting to jump in with solutions, but what if doing nothing was the answer?
🟪Think about it: When your child's emotions are volcanic, they can't listen to you. It's like trying to talk during a rock concert. Your words are just added noise to them.
🟩Instead, try this: Be present. Be physically close. Offer comfort through your eyes, through your familiar and reassuring facial features, through a gentle touch.
🟨They need you to be there, in the storm eye, with them.
🟩Turn on observation mode and listen with your heart, Dwell on those new voices you now hear.
🟨Giving space for the storm to pass, then connecting when they're ready. ♥️
