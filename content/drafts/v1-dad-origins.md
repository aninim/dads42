    >> Concept
      - Today's Dad embody the accumulation of positive attributes from all previous fatherhood roles [#Concept](eden://tag/306f276f-ec06-469c-9329-c9aac514cf5a)
        **What’s constant is a father’s love for his children – that likely existed in a cave 50,000 years ago as much as it does in a suburban home today. But how fathers express that love and what society expects from dads, has undoubtedly changed.**
        **For a modern fatherhood community, understanding this history is empowering. It reminds us that there’s no one “right” way to be a dad carved in stone – it’s a role that we as a society have shaped over time and continue to shape. Today’s dads have the chance to take the best lessons from the past (the dedication of the provider, the guidance of the teacher, the courage of the protector, the wisdom of the elder) and combine them with the best of the present (the equal partnership, the emotional openness, the playful engagement). In a sense, we’re the most well-equipped dads in history, with knowledge and tools our ancestors couldn’t dream of – from baby monitors to parenting books and, yes, the collective wisdom of “daddy Twitter” and forums.**
