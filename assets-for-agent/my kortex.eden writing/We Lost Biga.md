# We Lost Biga
We lost our pet dog two weeks ago, She was killed by a car crossing a highway road, or at least that what i figured out happened to her.
I was devestated, how can i let this happened! what does it say about me as a father?!
Biga was a Cnnan female dog, she was rescued with her mother when she was 2 months old, we adopted her when she was 3 months old from a foster family.
From the beginning we treated her as family, touching, carring, and warrying…
She was our first "child" just a few months time before our first born joind our family, she experienced with us everything from pregnancy to birth of our three boys, she was there with me during sleepless nights, with my wife when she needed company if i wasn't there, and also when i was there….she was a family member in our most exiting and meaningfull part of our lives.
I mornned for a whole week, had a hole in my chest, my mind was racing with "what ifs" and how could i have prevent this, why wasent i there to save her?, i never lost someone that was so close to me every day and night of the past 5 years.
After that horrible week, my thoughts changed, i started recalling terrible stories of kids of normative families losing their path in life ending up in dire situations.
This is my responsibility, the wellbeing of my family, whether they are near me or far away, this is on me to figure out. I must be there with them at all time even when not physically available.
This fathering thing just got much more seriosly, my kids will grow up and eventually roam on their own following their own uniqe path, and i wont be there for the most part, but i'm with them now, trully present, listenning and guiding. and this will make all the difference.
