>> Long Post
  **Modern dads are the universe’s “Best of Dads” mixtape.**
    Today’s dad embodies the accumulation of positive attributes from all previous fatherhood roles.
    What remains constant is a father’s love for his children—love that likely thrived 50,000 years ago in a cave just as it does in a suburban home today. Yet how fathers express that love, and the expectations society places on them, have undoubtedly changed.
    Now, we have the opportunity to take the best lessons from the past
    - The dedication of the provider
    - The guidance of the teacher
    - The courage of the protector
    - The wisdom of the elder
    …and combine them with the best of the present
    - Equal partnership
    - Emotional openness
    - Playful engagement
    In a sense, we’re the most well-equipped dads in history, armed with knowledge and tools our ancestors couldn’t have dreamed of.
