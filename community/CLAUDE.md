# community/ — Social Engine

Platform-specific playbooks. Where the community actually lives.

## Structure
- `skool/` — post seeds, module outlines, onboarding flow, weekly rhythm (Mission Monday / Wed AMA / Fri Wins)
- `email/` — welcome sequence (5 emails), nurture series, broadcast drafts
- `instagram/` — ManyChat comment-to-DM flows, captions, story scripts, bio variants

## Strategic anchors
- Skool is the **primary hub** — `skool.com/dads42-3659`
- ManyChat comment-to-DM is the **IG growth mechanic** (80% open rate, per playbook)
- Email list = owned audience, migrates off Skool algorithm risk
- Full strategy: `docs/The Dads42 launch playbook - zero to 100 members in 2026.md`

## Rules
- Every Skool post references brand voice + a Core Moment
- Email sequences follow the welcome flow in `email/welcome-sequence.md` — don't reinvent
- IG captions ≤ 125 chars for the "more" fold; first line = hook
- ManyChat flows documented before deploying — no undocumented funnels
