## Legal note

Training AI models on artistic references for private, non-commercial style study falls under research/fair-use in most jurisdictions. The trained LoRA produces NEW compositions that do not reproduce the reference images. Published outputs will be labeled as Dads'42 original work and will not reference or imitate specific pieces from the reference dataset.

If any artist's estate objects to their work being in the reference set, their images are removable and the LoRA is retrainable at ~$2 cost.
