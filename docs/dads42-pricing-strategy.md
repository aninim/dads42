**--- 06 --- LAUNCH SEQUENCE ---**

**How to Roll This Out**

Don\'t launch everything at once. Build anticipation, earn trust, then
open the door. Here\'s the sequence:

**Month 1 --- Plant the Flag**

Release the free Dad Origins Pack. 5 videos, manifesto PDF, 10
commandments carousel. All pointing to Skool. No selling yet. Pure
value. Get 50+ members.

*Goal: build the audience that will buy. Trust before transaction.*

**Month 2 --- Soft Launch**

Announce the \$27 Starter System to your existing Skool members and
email list first. \'You get it before anyone else.\' This is your
proof-of-concept sale. Target 20 buyers = \$540. More importantly --- 20
testimonials.

*Goal: first revenue, first feedback, first social proof.*

**Month 3 --- The Main Launch**

Open the \$97 Full Blueprint to the world. Use the testimonials from
Month 2. Run a 5-day launch window with urgency (price goes up, bonuses
expire). Email sequence, social posts, live Q&A.

*Goal: 20--30 sales = \$2,000--\$3,000 in a single week.*

**Month 4+ --- The Founding Circle**

Open your first live cohort. Max 20 dads. \$197 each. You run it for 5
weeks with weekly calls. This is your \$3,940 product. Run it quarterly.

*Goal: intimate, high-transformation experience that generates your best
testimonials ever.*

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  *\"The sequence matters as much as the price. Free → \$27 → \$97 →
  \$197 is not four products. It\'s one journey --- with a door at each
  step for a different type of dad.\"*

**--- 07 --- SALES PAGE FORMULA ---**

**How to Sell the \$97 Blueprint**

Every sales page for Dads\'42 follows the same emotional architecture.
Not features --- transformation.

**1. THE HOOK (above the fold)**

-   Headline: \'The Manual Nobody Handed You. Finally Written.\'

-   Sub: \'A 5-week system for the dad who figured out everything else
    himself --- and refuses to wing fatherhood.\'

-   CTA button: \'Get the Blueprint --- \$97\'

**2. THE MIRROR (they see themselves)**

-   You love your kids more than anything. But some nights you wonder if
    you\'re actually doing it right.

-   Nobody taught you this. Your own dad didn\'t model it. You\'re
    building the plane while flying it.

-   Every parenting account talks to someone else. Too soft. Too
    clinical. Too much.

**3. THE INTELLECTUAL EDGE (why this is different)**

-   Introduce Machin: \'Your brain physically rewired when you became an
    active dad. Evolution built you for this.\'

-   Introduce Adler: \'The self-doubt? The overcompensating? There\'s a
    name for it --- and a way through it.\'

-   Introduce Aurelius: \'A man who lost 5 children still showed up.
    Every single day. That\'s the standard.\'

**4. WHAT\'S INSIDE (the offer)**

-   Week by week breakdown. Visual. Clear. Specific.

-   Not \'you\'ll feel better\' --- \'by Week 3 you\'ll know exactly how
    to talk to your kids without shouting.\'

-   Show the workbooks, the frameworks, the bonus guide.

**5. WHO IT\'S FOR (pre-qualification)**

-   \'This is for you if\...\' --- the 5-item list from your landing
    page.

-   And crucially: \'This is NOT for you if\...\' --- dads who want a
    cheerleader, not a framework.

-   Pre-qualifying improves conversion AND community quality.

**6. THE CTA (one ask, strong close)**

-   \'You\'ve been figuring it out alone long enough. This is what
    changes that.\'

-   Price anchored: \'The Founding Circle members paid \$197. The
    Blueprint is \$97. For now.\'

-   Guarantee: \'If you go through Week 1 and don\'t feel it was worth
    every cent, I\'ll refund you.\'

*\"The blueprint exists. Now put a price on it.\"*
