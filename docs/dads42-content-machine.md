**--- 01 --- THE CORE PHILOSOPHY ---**

**The Machine, Not the Muse**

Waiting for inspiration is why most creators disappear. The machine
doesn\'t wait. It runs. Here\'s the philosophy underneath everything
that follows:

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **ONE RAW SESSION = ONE WEEK OF CONTENT**

  You record or write one \'raw source\' --- a 5-minute talking head, a
  voice note, a stream-of-consciousness paragraph about something that
  happened this week as a dad. Everything else is extracted from that
  source. You don\'t create content seven times. You create once and
  slice it seven ways.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **IDEAS COME FROM YOUR PAIN BANK, NOT THE INTERNET**

  Your best content is already inside you. The moment you lost your
  temper. The night you sat in the car instead of going inside. The thing
  your kid said that cracked you open. These are inexhaustible. The
  Content Engine and the 6 angles exist to turn those moments into posts
  --- fast.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **EVERY PIECE SERVES THE MACHINE**

  Nothing is posted for posting\'s sake. Every reel, every carousel,
  every written post has one job: move a dad one step closer to Skool.
  The content machine is a funnel disguised as a community. It feels like
  a conversation. It works like a pipeline.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  **CONSISTENCY BEATS QUALITY EVERY TIME --- UNTIL IT DOESN\'T**

  In Month 1, showing up matters more than being perfect. Post the
  imperfect reel. Publish the rough carousel. The algorithm rewards
  frequency. Your audience rewards authenticity. But by Month 3, the
  machine is running smoothly and quality starts to compound.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**--- 02 --- THE WEEKLY ENGINE ---**

**The 2-Hour Content Session**

This is your entire content operation for the week. Two sessions. One
framework. Everything that gets posted flows from this single time
block.

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------
  *\"Two hours feels like not enough. That\'s the point. Constraints
  force decisions. Decisions create content. Unlimited time creates
  paralysis.\"*

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------

**SESSION 1 --- The Raw Source (60--75 mins)**

This is where everything begins. One raw piece of content that becomes
the week\'s fuel.

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:00--0:10 **PHASE 1**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Brain Dump**                                                        |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *Don\'t think. Don\'t edit. Just pull from the pain bank.*            |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** Open a blank doc or voice note app                             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** Ask: \'What happened this week as a dad that I can\'t stop     |
| thinking about?\'                                                     |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** Write or speak for 10 minutes without stopping --- raw,        |
| unfiltered, honest                                                    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** This becomes your source material. One story, one moment, one  |
| frustration, one win.                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:10--0:40 **PHASE 2**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Record the Raw Source**                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *Pick your format for this week\'s anchor piece. Rotate over time.*   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** OPTION A --- Talking Head (5--7 mins): Set up camera, speak    |
| directly to a dad at 11pm. Hook first. No script --- just the         |
| framework: Hook → Problem → Evidence → Solution → CTA                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** OPTION B --- Voice Note (10 mins): Record yourself explaining  |
| the week\'s insight while walking. Raw audio becomes captions + a     |
| written post.                                                         |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** OPTION C --- Written Draft (500 words):                        |
| Stream-of-consciousness. Your most honest, unfiltered version. Don\'t |
| polish --- that comes later.                                          |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** OPTION D --- Carousel Script (10 slides): Write the 10 slides  |
| as bullet points first. Slide 1 is always the hook. Slide 10 is       |
| always the CTA.                                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:40--0:75 **PHASE 3**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Extract the Assets**                                                |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *One raw source → multiple pieces. This is where the machine pays     |
| off.*                                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** Pull the 3 best lines or moments from your raw source ---      |
| these become standalone hooks                                         |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** Identify the core insight --- this becomes the carousel        |
| headline                                                              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** Find the most relatable moment --- this becomes the reel hook  |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** Spot the philosophical angle (Machin / Adler / Aurelius) ---   |
| this becomes the \'hot take\' post                                    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **5.** Write your CTA for the week (always points to Skool or the     |
| current lead magnet)                                                  |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

**SESSION 2 --- The Production Run (45--60 mins)**

Content doesn\'t exist until it\'s formatted, captioned, and scheduled.
This session turns your raw assets into publishable posts.

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:00--0:20 **PHASE 1**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Format & Polish**                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *Apply the Dads\'42 voice filter to everything you extracted in       |
| Session 1.*                                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** Edit the talking head down to 60--90 seconds for Reels/TikTok  |
| (keep the rest for YouTube)                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** Write 3 caption variants --- one direct, one story-led, one    |
| question-based                                                        |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** Design the carousel in Canva using the dark editorial template |
| (or hand to a designer)                                               |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** Format the written post --- trim to 150--200 words, bold the   |
| hook line, end with the CTA                                           |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:20--0:45 **PHASE 2**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Schedule the Week**                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *Batch scheduling once = zero daily decision fatigue.*                |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** Load all posts into your scheduler (Later, Buffer, or Meta     |
| Business Suite)                                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** Follow the 5-day cadence (see Weekly Calendar section)         |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** Write one Skool community post from the same raw source ---    |
| this week\'s discussion prompt                                        |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** Set reminders to respond to comments within the first hour of  |
| posting (algorithm boost)                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| 0:45--0:60 **PHASE 3**                                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Feed the Pipeline**                                                 |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| *10 minutes that compound. Every week.*                               |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **1.** Add 3 new ideas to your Pain Bank doc (moments, frustrations,  |
| wins from the week)                                                   |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **2.** Note which post performed best --- what angle, what format,    |
| what hook                                                             |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **3.** Pick next week\'s anchor format (rotate to avoid creative      |
| fatigue)                                                              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **4.** Close the laptop. The machine is running.                      |

**--- 04 --- THE FORMAT PLAYBOOK ---**

**4 Formats, One Voice**

You said yes to all four formats. Good --- that\'s the machine working.
Each format has a specific job, a specific production time, and a
specific hook formula. Master these four and you\'ll never stare at a
blank screen again.

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **🎥 TALKING HEAD VIDEO** · 20--30 mins to record + edit              |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Best for:** Identity hooks, confessional moments, hot takes with    |
| your face behind them                                                 |
|                                                                       |
| **Hook formula:** *\[Provocative statement\]. \[Pause.\] Here\'s what |
| I mean.*                                                              |
|                                                                       |
| **Example:** *\'Aka Pygmy dads spend 47% of their day within arm\'s   |
| reach of their infants. They\'ve been doing it for 50,000 years. You  |
| didn\'t invent involved fatherhood. You just forgot it.\'*            |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **🎞️ VOICE OVER B-ROLL** · 15--20 mins total                          |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Best for:** Tactical wins, research-backed content, anything where  |
| the visuals carry the emotion                                         |
|                                                                       |
| **Hook formula:** *You thought \[X\]. Science says \[Y\]. Here\'s     |
| what that means for you tonight.*                                     |
|                                                                       |
| **Example:** *\'You thought being strict was the opposite of being    |
| warm. Adler proved a century ago that control breeds defiance --- and |
| what actually builds respect. Here\'s the move.\'*                    |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **📊 CAROUSEL / SLIDE POST** · 20--25 mins to write + design          |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Best for:** Tactical lists, frameworks, the 10 Commandments, the 6  |
| angles, research breakdowns                                           |
|                                                                       |
| **Hook formula:** *Slide 1: Bold provocative claim. Slides 2--9: One  |
| insight per slide. Slide 10: CTA to Skool.*                           |
|                                                                       |
| **Example:** *Slide 1: \'The Industrial Revolution invented the       |
| absent dad. It was only 200 years ago. Here\'s what came before.\' →  |
| 9 slides of historical evidence → Slide 10: \'Join the men            |
| remembering.\'*                                                       |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **✍️ WRITTEN POST** · 10--15 mins                                     |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Best for:** Confessional stories, hot takes, Us vs. Them            |
| positioning, philosophical reflections                                |
|                                                                       |
| **Hook formula:** *One sentence hook. Two sentences of tension.       |
| Resolution or question. Three-line CTA.*                              |
|                                                                       |
| **Example:** *\'I used to think yelling meant I was failing as a      |
| dad.\\nTurns out Marcus Aurelius lost five children and still showed  |
| up the next morning asking himself the same question.\\nYou\'re not   |
| failing. You\'re in the oldest conversation in human                  |
| history.\\n\\nJoin 200 dads having it out loud → \[link in bio\]\'*   |

**--- 09 --- FREEZE BREAKERS ---**

**When You Go Blank**

Every creator freezes. The machine has a protocol for that. When you
open the laptop and feel nothing --- run one of these. Pick the first
one that doesn\'t make you want to close the laptop again.

+---+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| * | **THE 3-MINUTE VOICE NOTE**                                       |
| * |                                                                   |
| 1 | Set your phone to voice memo. Ask yourself one question out loud  |
| * | and answer it for 3 minutes without stopping. Don\'t edit. Just   |
| * | talk. The question: \'What happened this week as a dad that I\'m  |
|   | still thinking about?\' That 3 minutes is your raw source.        |
|   | Transcribe it, extract the hook, write the post.                  |
+---+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

+---+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| * | **THE PAIN BANK SHUFFLE**                                         |
| * |                                                                   |
| 2 | Open your Pain Bank. Read the Wound Vault. Find the entry that    |
| * | still stings a little. That sting is the post. Write one sentence |
| * | about that moment. Then ask: what would I want another dad to     |
|   | know if he was in that exact moment right now? That answer is the |
|   | content.                                                          |
+---+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

+---+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
