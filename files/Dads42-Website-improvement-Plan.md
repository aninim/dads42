# Dads42 Website Improvement Plan

## 1. Hero Section (Above the Fold)

**Headline:** Nobody handed you the manual. We built one.

**Subheadline:** Practical tools, live calls, and a private room of dads who actually get it.

**Primary CTA Button:** Join Free on Skool

**Secondary CTA:** Download Dad Origins Pack (Free)

**Visual:** Large authentic photo or illustration — a dad with his son(s), grounded and real, not stock-photo cheesy. Engineer-style workshop background optional.

**Micro-animation:** The number "42" subtly pulses. Headline text fades in on load.

## 2. Lead Magnet & Email Funnel

**Lead Magnet:** Dad Origins Pack — Manifesto + The 10 Commandments (PDF)

**Form Placement:** Directly below hero, clearly visible.

**Welcome Email Sequence (5 emails):**
- Email 1: Thank you + instant download link
- Email 2: Why most dad advice fails (your story)
- Email 3: First live topic preview (Identity After Kids)
- Email 4: Quick win from the community
- Email 5: Personal invite to join Skool + Founding Father link

**Social Proof:** 3–4 short testimonials with headshots placed right under the form.

## 3. Visual & Animation Upgrades

- Replace long text blocks with icon cards for "What's Inside"
- Add 60–90 second explainer video on homepage (you on camera or simple animation)
- Mobile-first design — fast loading critical for busy dads
- Clean card-based layout for the 10 starter topics
- Subtle scroll animations on feature sections
